My story

Moringa School came to my attention during my time freelancing as an Architect I was looking for a coding bootcamp to accelerate my learning and get a clear roadmap in this new exciting endeavour in learning.

I have always loved creating and building things: from the cardboard houses I use to make for my dolls when I was little to the construction projects I have undertaken as an Architect and most recently the amazing applications I have built in Moringa. My favourite application being Squad which is an eSports social gaming application, built in Django that will go a long way in promoting eSports in Kenya. A close second is Vybe which is an Flask application that facilitates planning trips to rarely visited places within the country.

In a span of 6 months I have learnt frontend technologies, web frameworks including Angular, Django and Flask, relational databases and APIs not to mention but a few.

My time in Moringa has been transformative to say the least. It is a practical, fast-paced and fun environment that really pushes the assumed limits you place on yourself.
